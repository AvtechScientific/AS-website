Our Fields of Expertise

  • Delivering full commercial consulting, training and integration services for our open-source Advanced Simulation Library (ASL).
  • Undertaking contracted development projects for ASL, ranging from adding a single new feature, to writing entire applications and software suites.
  • Providing Virtual Engineering solutions: Virtual Prototyping, Virtual Manufacturing, Virtual Testing.
